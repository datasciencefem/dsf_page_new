What’s Data Science FEM?
Data Science FEM is a Spanish speaking LATAM community, focused to promote data science knowledge with woman within in the region. We provide scholarships and technical education programs to our female members -mainly, but non gender restricted-
As a community, we have 3 key goals:

  • Contributing with the economic development of our countries, while helping the pivot from primary resource’s exportation to a high added value product and services in technology.
  • Reduce gender pay gap, using education as a way to develop high demand technical skills, so our members can have more and better opportunities to increasing their life quality and contributing within their region development.
  • Promote STEM studies in women to reduce gender gap in technology and promoting a diverse and inclusive society.
